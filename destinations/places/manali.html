Magical Manali

04 nights / 05 days

Day 1 - Arrival at Chandigarh & Drive to Manali

Arrive at Chandigarh Airport and you will proceed to Manali. On arrival, check-in at the hotel. Overnight in Manali.

Day 2 - Manali Sightseeing

Today go on an adventurous drive to Solang valley. All above mentioned adventure activities can be availed here at Solang Valley. Later, today visit popular Hadimba Temple, Naggar Castle and Art gallery. In the evening, go for a walk along Mall Road. Overnight in Manali.

Day 3 - Manali - Day at leisure

After breakfast, enjoy full day at leisure in Manali. Alternatively, you may explore the destination offering numerous adventure sports and other places of interest. Overnight stay in Manali

Day 4 - Manali - Chandigarh

After breakfast, drive towards Chandigarh and check-in at the hotel. If time permits, visit Nek Chand's Rock Garden. Overnight stay in Chandigarh.

Day 5 - Chandigarh - Departure

After breakfast, you will be transferred to Chandigarh airport for retune.
